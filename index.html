About

Founded and run by Peter Svensson.

Sparetimecoders is run by Peter Svensson (he/him), a senior software architect and engineer based in Gothenburg, Sweden, and educated at Chalmers University of Technology. Years of building, leading, and mentoring across product and platform teams — with a steady focus on shipping clean, observable, well-tested systems.

The work spans cloud-native backends, developer-experience platforms, and team-level coaching on continuous delivery and modern engineering practice. The aim is simple: leave teams faster, calmer, and more confident in production.
